Our Beginning

A Passion Born from Love

What started as a simple passion for capturing beautiful moments has evolved into a legacy of excellence spanning over 15 years. At 35 Frames Photography, we believe that every photograph tells a unique story—your story.

Founded in 2010 by award-winning photographer Rajesh Kumar, our journey began with a single camera and an unwavering commitment to preserving life's most precious memories. Today, we're proud to be one of the most sought-after luxury photography studios, trusted by clients across USA, UK, Europe, and UAE.

Our philosophy is simple yet profound: every click of the shutter should capture not just an image, but an emotion, a feeling, a moment that will be cherished for generations to come. This dedication to excellence has earned us 25+ prestigious awards and the trust of over 500 satisfied clients worldwide.

Our Mission

To capture the essence of life's most precious moments with artistic vision, technical excellence, and heartfelt dedication. We strive to create photographs that become cherished family heirlooms, preserving memories for generations to come.

Our Vision

To be the world's most trusted luxury photography brand, recognized for transforming ordinary moments into extraordinary art. We envision a future where every family treasures professionally captured memories that tell their unique story.

Our Values

Excellence, integrity, creativity, and compassion guide everything we do. We believe in building lasting relationships with our clients, understanding their vision, and exceeding their expectations with every single photograph we create.
